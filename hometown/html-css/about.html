Lethbridge

Lethbridge, Alberta is a scenic town with many different places to visit. Whether you are here to enjoy the view from the coulees or to see the expansive shopping districts of downtown, Lethbridge is the place for you. A growing city with both a college and a university, many people come to Lethbridge to achieve a higher education as research that can change the world is conducted within these facilities, as well as welcome the next generation into a rapidly changing society.

With a diverse culture, history and roots in many parts of Canada, Lethbridge is the place to be. Lethbridge is the home of the Viaduct High Level Bridge, the longest in the world to date, and spans the beautiful Old Man River across multiple hills and valleys known as coulees.

The city is very bright and sunny, receiving sunlight most days of the year to help warm the cold winters, and bring a sense of joy to the residents.

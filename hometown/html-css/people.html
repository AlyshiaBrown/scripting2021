People

The citizens of Lethbridge are of a diverse age range, from college and university students to senior citizens from various cultures and groups. Lethbridge is located on Blackfoot Territory and has a prominent First Nations history behind it.

Other members of the community are here to learn and teach, as the educational institutions of Lethbridge are one of the main draws to this peaceful city.
